Argentina

Requirements

  • Have a couple years experience leading technical teams.
  • Be good at coaching teammates in all phases of their career.
  • Enjoy working in iterative development environments.
  • Know how to focus teams to achieve their goals.
  • Have enough technical background to help a team evaluate trade-offs during development.
  • Work well with technical and non-technical people to deliver high-value results.
  • Like to lead by example.

The current stack focuses on Node, ElasticSearch, MongoDB, and React, but is open to other technologies.
